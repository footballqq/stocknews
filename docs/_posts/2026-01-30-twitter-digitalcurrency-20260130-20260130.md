- **Cross‑Agency Coordination:**  The CFTC‑SEC joint initiative underscores a move toward consolidated oversight, which should reduce regulatory arbitrage and improve market integrity.  This is likely to encourage larger funds to allocate to crypto‑exposed strategies, especially those that can demonstrate compliance‑ready operations.

### 2.2  Commodity & Inflation Dynamics  
- **Metals Surge:**  Gold and silver have exhibited parabolic moves, outpacing traditional equity indices.  The price action is being interpreted as a blend of inflation‑hedge demand and speculative positioning.  The correlation with crypto is shifting—while Bitcoin has been under pressure, metals are increasingly viewed as “digital‑gold” alternatives in a risk‑off environment.  
- **Base‑Metal Momentum:**  Copper, nickel, and other industrial metals are rallying on expectations of sustained demand from renewable‑energy rollouts and supply constraints in key mining regions.  This macro backdrop reinforces a broader “commodities‑first” narrative that is influencing asset‑allocation decisions across both traditional and crypto‑centric portfolios.
